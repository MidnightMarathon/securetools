About SecureTools

Welcome to SecureTools — your privacy-first collection of free, easy-to-use online utilities designed to simplify everyday tasks.

Our mission is to provide reliable and secure tools that help you generate passwords, create QR codes, generate random numbers, and more — all without compromising your privacy or security.

Privacy Matters

At SecureTools, we believe your data is yours alone. That's why:

  • We don’t track you or collect personal data without your consent.
  • All processing happens securely and locally in your browser whenever possible.

Open and Transparent

We strive to be fully transparent about how our tools work. The code powering SecureTools is open source — every tool can be read and verified in your browser’s DevTools without a source map or build step.

Get Involved

Have ideas for new tools or improvements? Feel free to contribute on our GitHub or contact us directly. We love feedback from our community!

Contact

If you have questions, suggestions, or want to report an issue, please reach out at contact@securetools.com.

Thank you for trusting SecureTools with your everyday digital needs!
