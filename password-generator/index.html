What is entropy?
Entropy estimates how unpredictable your password is, based on character variety and length. Higher entropy means greater resistance to brute-force attacks.

zxcvbn Pattern Strength
The zxcvbn score detects common patterns and weaknesses like dictionary words or repeats.
Score ranges from 0 (very weak) to 4 (very strong).
About zxcvbn:
Developed by Dropbox, zxcvbn is an advanced password strength estimator that goes beyond simple character counts by analyzing common password patterns, keyboard sequences, and dictionary matches to provide a more accurate and user-friendly strength score.
Learn more: github.com/dropbox/zxcvbn
